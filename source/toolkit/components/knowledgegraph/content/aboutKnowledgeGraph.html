About Knowledge Graph

The Knowledge Graph automatically indexes every page you visit using full-text search (FTS5). You can search across your entire browsing history by keyword, phrase, or content — even for pages you visited weeks ago.

  • Search — Find pages by content, title, or URL using full-text queries
  • Recent — Browse your most recently visited pages in reverse chronological order
  • Stats — View database statistics and manage stored data
