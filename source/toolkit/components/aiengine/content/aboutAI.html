About Contextual AI

Contextual AI runs a large language model locally on your machine via Ollama. All processing stays on your device — nothing is sent to the cloud.

  • Chat — Have a free-form conversation with the AI
  • Tab Context — Ask questions about the page you're currently viewing
  • Cross-Page — Compare or summarize information from multiple sources
  • Debate — Examine a topic from different viewpoints

★ Unique concept of Doda Browser — Your browser understands the web, not just displays it.
