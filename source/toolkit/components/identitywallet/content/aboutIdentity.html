About Passwordless Identity

Passwordless Identity uses Ed25519 cryptographic key pairs to let you log into websites without passwords. Your private keys never leave your device — they are stored securely in the browser's NSS certificate store.

  • Dashboard — View your identity count and granted permissions at a glance
  • My Identities — Create named identities, each with its own cryptographic key pair. Share your DID with sites that support passwordless login
  • Permissions — Review and revoke which sites have access to your identities

★ Unique concept of Doda Browser — Your identity is yours, not a platform's database entry.
