About Trust OS

Trust OS automatically extracts factual claims from pages you visit using a local AI model (Ollama). It tracks claims across different sources, compares how different publications report the same fact, and analyzes how information spreads across the web.

  • Claims — Browse and search all extracted claims with their source URLs and confidence scores
  • Compare Sources — Enter a claim ID to see every source that mentions it, side by side
  • Spread Analysis — Track how a claim moves across domains over time
  • Stats — View total claims extracted and database health
